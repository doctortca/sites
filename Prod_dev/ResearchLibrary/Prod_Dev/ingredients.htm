Simply put, WBT is training deployed over the Internet. WBT is the modern descendant of CBT, or Computer-based training. Many of the traditional methods and models for developing CBT apply to WBT. But unlike CBT, WBT must take into account the bandwidth constraints of the Internet. It borrows heavily from the rules and standards already present in traditional Web site design.

So what makes good WBT? All of the following should be addressed to develop effective WBT:

  • interactivity design
  • interface & usability design
  • instructional strategy
  • adult learning theory
  • media
  • development staff (Adobe PDF document)
