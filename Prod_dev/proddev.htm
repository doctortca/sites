Welcome to the Product Development group's Web site. This site's goal is to provide information about the Product Development team and its development methodologies and tools.

We hope you'll find this site to be a valuable resource. The latest in Web-based training (WBT) research, methodologies, and procedures can be found here, as well as hot topics surrounding distance education. 

The Product Development team's goals are as follows:

  1. Develop online and new digital media content
  2. Convert existing content to online & digital formats
  3. Set benchmarks, policies and procedures for all online and digital content development
  4. Research new tools and methods for content development and delivery
  5. Be the streaming media driver
  6. Take TV online and digital

The primary focus of the Product Development group is research & development. Visit the areas below for details on our R&D efforts:

  • Design
  • Processes
  • Standards
  • Tools
