Each of our homes comes standard with a variety of features. We also offer our Exclusive Line of homes, which adds only a small portion to the price of your home and allows you the freedom to select all sorts of custom options at a fraction of the cost.

Standard Features

Standard with every Home Sweet Home design, are the following features:

  • 10-year manufacturer's warranty
  • fully sodded front yard
  • brick front porch
  • divided light windows on front of home
  • 36 " fireplace with wood mantle
  • designer brass fixtures in baths and kitchen
  • designer wallpaper in kitchen and baths
  • energy efficient appliances
  • security system

Exclusive Line

In addition to the Standard Features listed above, with an Exclusive Line home, you'll also receive the following:
