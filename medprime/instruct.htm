Course Instructions

  1. View the Syllabus

    Before beginning this course, you are required to read through the course syllabus.

    You can view the syllabus at any time while taking the course. Simply click the Syllabus button on the navigation bar below.

  2. Watch the Video

    Most of this course consists of video content. The video is broken into logical sections and will occasionally stop at key points.

    When the video stops, you'll be prompted to click the Next button to advance to the next section. Occasionally, you may be asked to answer quiz questions before proceeding.

  3. Answer Quiz Questions

    If there are quiz questions to answer, you'll see Question appear in this area of the screen.

    When you finish answering quiz questions, you'll be prompted to click Play on the video window again to watch another video clip. Or if you're at the end of the course, you'll be prompted to take the test associated with this course.

  4. Take the Test

    Once you are finished taking this course, you will be provided a link to the eCademy, where you can take the assessment associated with the course.

    Exiting the Course

If you choose to exit the course at any time without taking the test, click the Exit button on the navigation bar below.
